Application Period is Open!

The GovLab, in partnership with the USDA’s Office of the Chief Information Officer, is sponsoring the 2016 USDA Open Data Science, Technology, Engineering, Agriculture and Math (STEAM) Summer Camp.

The camp will run from July 11 – August 5, 2016, featuring two cohorts of students ages 12-14 and 15-17. Students 12-14 will attend from July 11th – July 23rd and students ages15-17 from July 25th – August 5th. The District of Columbia Public School Children are the priority candidates.

The objective of the camp is to provide students with the opportunity to learn about the Mission of USDA and to accomplish various projects by using USDA open data and crowdsourcing to assist in the decision making process on critical projects and programs.

The themes for this year’s camp are Urban Agriculture, Urban Forestry and Food Safety.

The Camp will be held at the U.S. Department of Agriculture Headquarters located at 1400 Independence Avenue, SW. Washington, DC, 20250. Tuition and lunches are free.

Please email applications to: usdasummerprogram@thegovlab.org. All applications must be received by COB May 20, 2016.

For additional questions or concerns, please contact Cynthia Larkins at 202-720-7232, or email at: Cynthia.Larkins@wdc.usda.gov
